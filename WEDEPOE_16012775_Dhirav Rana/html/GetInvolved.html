Get Involved

  • ABOUT WESSA MEMBERSHIP

    WESSA is a membership-based organisation and, throughout South Africa, our members initiate environmental projects and organise educational events and activities in their local communities. By joining WESSA, you will be helping to provide valuable support for these volunteer initiatives and will be part of a growing environmental network operating at a grassroots community level via our branches, partners, business members, affiliates and friends groups.

  • WESSA membership work to:

    Protect and conserve natural habitats and biodiversity Engage with individuals and communities to enable them to live more sustainably Create opportunities for social and economic upliftment through environmental education and skills development Collaborate and network with groups and organisations that have similar objectives Raise awareness and promote public participation around important environmental issues
