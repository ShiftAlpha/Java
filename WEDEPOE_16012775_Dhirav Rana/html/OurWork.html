Our Work

  • Schools:

    We are a national leader in environmental education, investing in youth and facilitating transformation in schools through innovative programmes that offer project-based learning. We work with schools and teachers throughout South Africa to support and improve school curricula with regard to environmental learning, equipping children to live sustainably in the future.

  • Work Skills:

    We provide effective training, education and skills development programmes to private individuals, corporates and all spheres of government, with an extensive range of courses designed to address the critical scarcity of the skills that are required to drive the green and blue economy. We are registered as an accredited training provider with the ETDPSETA, LGSETA and CATHSSETA. Our courses place emphasis on developing hands-on, practical skills, exploring situated knowledge and instilling a sense of pride and purpose. In 2017 1,150 participants attended our accredited and non-accredited courses facilitated around the country. We partner effectively with corporates too, helping them meet their CSI goals by delivering training on their behalf, for the benefit of both the environment and disadvantaged communities.

  • Eco-Tourism:

    We are actively working to promote the development of Sustainable Tourism in South Africa. Since 2001 we have managed Blue Flag - the longest running and most recognisable eco-label in the world - resulting in improved tourism facilities at over 70 different sites along our country's beautiful coastline. The Tourism Blue Flag project, in partnership with the National Department of Tourism, is improving the experience of local and international visitors to our beaches while simultaneously developing youth for employment. In 2016 we introduced the international Green Key eco-label - a leading standard of excellence in the field of environmental responsibility and sustainable operations within the tourism industry.

  • Education Centres:

    We operate three coastal and inland environmental education centres in KwaZulu-Natal and one centre in Limpopo, providing four unique and diverse educational experiences in an outdoor classroom environment. Well-maintained accommodation and dining facilities are available at each centre and our onsite, professional staff facilitate outdoor adventure activities and programmes on environmental awareness, leadership and human capacity development. In addition to providing exciting education programmes for school groups, the centres are ideal training venues for tertiary level courses too. The Umgeni Valley Nature Reserve and Twinstreams centres offer attractive accommodation options for tourists and ideal bases for exploring onsite and local attractions in the Midlands and Zululand.
